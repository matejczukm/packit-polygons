How to Play

Polygonal PackIt

  1. A tile can be placed on turn i if it is made up of i or i+1 hexagons and becomes a convex polygon after edge smoothing.
  2. The player who places the last tile wins the game.
  3. To create a tile, select each corner in the correct order, so they can be connected with horizontal or tilted vertical lines. To finish the outline, click on the already selected corner to trigger the tile's fill.
    Once the tile is correctly formed, confirm the move by clicking the Confirm move button.
    If you want to cancel the move, use the Undo move button.
